From Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA 1 & 2)

Options of bus or KLIA Express train to KL Sentral station, switch train (1 stop) to Pasar Seni and we are right opposite the station

From Sultan Abdul Aziz Shah (Subang Airport):

Take the local bus to Pasar Seni Station. (We are located right opposite Pasar Seni Station)

From Pudu Sentral Bus Terminal :

Walking distance (please see Chinatown Map)

From KL Sentral :

Switch train (1 stop) to Pasar Seni and we are right opposite the station

From Bandar Tasik Selatan bus terminal :

Take the train to KL Sentral station, switch train (1 stop) to Pasar Seni and we are right opposite the station
